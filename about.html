About Us

Top 100 Global Universities

Outstanding Research in Technology and Humanities

Leadership in Sustainability and Green Initiatives

Founded in 1895, Cedar Vally University has been a leader in higher education for over a century. What began as a small regional college has grown into a globally recognized institution with a diverse student body representing over 100 countries. Our mission has always been to provide access to world-class education, emphasizing inclusivity, academic rigor, and community impact. Today, with more than 20,000 students, we continue to uphold the values that have defined us for generations while evolving to meet the demands of a rapidly changing world.

Throughout our rich history, Cedar Vally has consistently pioneered advancements in education and research. In the early 20th century, we expanded our curriculum to include emerging fields like engineering, science, and the arts. During challenging times, including the Great Depression and two World Wars, we remained committed to providing opportunities for veterans and underprivileged students. Our reputation for resilience and innovation was cemented during these times, and we became known for pushing the boundaries of knowledge across multiple disciplines.

By the mid-20th century, Cedar Vally University was at the forefront of groundbreaking research, contributing to key advancements in technology, medicine, and the humanities. Our global partnerships and research centers further expanded our influence, allowing students and faculty to collaborate on projects with global significance. Today, our cutting-edge programs and interdisciplinary approach ensure that students are not only knowledgeable but equipped with practical skills to lead in their respective fields.

At Cedar Vally, education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus life is a hub for intellectual and cultural exchange, offering over 200 student organizations that foster leadership, creativity, and community engagement. Our faculty, composed of leading scholars and industry experts, provide students with mentorship that encourages critical thinking and innovation. The University's mix of historic charm and modern facilities creates an environment where students can thrive academically and socially.

Looking ahead, Cedar Vally University remains committed to preparing the next generation of global leaders. We continue to expand our initiatives in sustainability, technology, and social justice, ensuring that our graduates are equipped to address the challenges of the future. Our alumni, who lead in various sectors around the world, are a testament to the transformative power of a Silvergate education. We invite you to be part of our journey as we continue to shape the future of education and society.
